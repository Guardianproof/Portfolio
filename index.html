Strategic Approach

  • Chat Engagement – activating real chatters and raiders to maintain daily discussions and fast replies.
  • Point-Based System – rewarding members weekly for chatting, raiding, inviting, and event participation with roles, tokens, WLs, or NFTs.
  • Community Events – running weekly events like quizzes, meme battles, AMAs, and recap days to build hype and routine.
  • Contests & Virality – launching meme and shill contests with real rewards and cross-platform exposure.
  • Twitter Optimization – daily tweets: educational, memes, alpha, callouts, and spotlight posts. Applying for verification & X Communities.
  • Automation & Infrastructure – setting up bots for spam protection, leaderboards, event reminders, and smooth onboarding.
  • Moderator System – rotating mod teams to manage chats, assist users, and support event flow – with performance-based rewards.
  • Ambassador Program – engaging 50+ micro-influencers with weekly tasks, tracking performance and rewarding top promoters.
  • Analytics & Optimization – weekly reports on activity, raids, contributors, and campaign success to improve continuously.
